# Product Guidelines

## Brand Messaging & Tone
- **Professional & Technical:** Focus on technical precision and engineering excellence to build trust and reliability in security operations.
- **Collaborative & Community-Driven:** Emphasize open standards, sharing of security workflows, and community participation.
- **Minimalist & Efficiency-Oriented:** Highlight speed, simplicity, and the "Security-as-Code" vision by providing a lean and powerful operational layer.

## Visual Identity
- **Terminal-First Design:** Prioritize high-contrast, text-based clarity to ensure optimal usability in CLI and terminal environments.
- **Structured & Grid-Based:** Use a clean and organized layout to reflect the structured nature of standardized workflows and normalized security data.
- **Minimalism:** Avoid visual clutter and focus strictly on essential information, ensuring a distraction-free experience for security professionals.

## Prose Style
- **Clear & Concise:** Use direct and unambiguous language to describe security tasks and results.
- **Technical Precision:** Use standardized cybersecurity terminology (e.g., CVE, CWE, CVSS) correctly and consistently.
- **Evidence-Driven:** Ensure all documentation and reports are backed by clear evidence and data sources.
